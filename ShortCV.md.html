IIIF + Maps Community

Role: Co-founder/Co-Chair

https://iiif.io/community/groups/maps/
This group works on defining best practice in associating geographic data with IIIF materials. This includes IIIF recipes but also more in depth work to align efforts to link IIIF maps to geospatial systems. The group recently succeeded bringing navPlace, and extension for associating spatial data with IIIF digital object, to approval. https://preview.iiif.io/api/navplace_extension/api/extension/navplace/
